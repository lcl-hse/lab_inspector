The diagram illustrates three different graphs which show the proportion of population aged 65 and over from 1940 to 2040 in Japan, Sweden and USA. It can be seen that the overall trend is upward. 
Initially, the indexes of three countries were located on the various positions and the Japan had a lowest mark among them of just 5%. There was a slight decrease in the proportions of people from Japan aged 65 and over between 1940 to 1980 while other countries indexes showed growth. 
After 1980s the situation changed. From 1980 to 2030 Japanese percentage was growing constantly, USA's marks stayed on the same level and indexes of Sweden were fluctuating. In 2040 the biggest proportion was shown by Japan with approximately 27%.
