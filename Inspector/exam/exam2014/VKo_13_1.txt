The graph shows the levels of post-school qualifications in Australia and the proportion of men and women who attended them in 1999. 
It can be clearly seen that the majority of males got the skilled vocational diploma, while the majority of females got undergraduate diploma. 
The biggest difference between females and males in getting qualifications is in getting skilled vocational diploma, where the 10% of females and 90% of males got it. In contrast there was not much difference in getting bachelor's degree: graph shows that 45% of males and 55% of females held it! 
Speaking about the proportion of females and males in getting qualifications we should consider that males had the biggest percent in getting skilled vocational diploma, postgraduate diploma and master's degree. Differ from men, the percent of females was bigger in getting undergraduate diploma and bachelor's degree. In addition, the majority of females in 1999 in Australia got undergraduate diploma.
