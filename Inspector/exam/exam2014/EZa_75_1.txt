The chart provides information about post-school qualifications in Australia in 1999. 
It can be seen that there is a big difference between men and women, who had vocational diploma. The percentage of females was about 20% while males had approximately 95%. However, the ammount of females, who had undergraduate diploma and bachelor's degree was bigger than ammount of males. About 70% of women had undergraduate diploma and about 50% had bachelor's degree. The proportion of men was different. More males had bachelor's degree than undergraduate diploma. The percentage of men who got postgraduate diploma is bigger than percentage f women. The same trend can be seen among people who got master's degree. 
To sum up, males had more qualifications. However, there were stages, for example undergraduate diploma, where the ammount of women was bigger.
