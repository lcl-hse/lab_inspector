The picture demonstrates us situation with ages of populations between 2000 and 2050 in Yemen and Italy. Therefore we can see what was in the past and what will be in future, some predictions. 
The first two pictures in the left corner is about Yemen and Italy past data about ages. As we can see, there are a lot of teenagers in Yemen, more than 50 percent. However young people between 0 and 14 years were not so big part of Italians. The main group of Italy people in 2000 included 15-59 years old resident. 
Pie charts about future situation in two countries tell us about decrease in number of people aged 15-59 years in Italy. It will still be a biggest part of all residents, but it will be almost the same with 60+ years old. On the other side, the middle-age part of Yemen's people will improve their position in 2050.
