As for age groop of 15-59 years, in Yemen it increases from 46,3% to 57,3%. At the same time in Italy the part of this age category falls for about 15% to 46,2% in 2000. Moreother there is a huge difference between part of 60+ years people. In Yemen they took only 3,6% in 2050 and rise to 5,7% in 2050, but in Italy this groop tends to grow from 24,1% to more than 42%.
