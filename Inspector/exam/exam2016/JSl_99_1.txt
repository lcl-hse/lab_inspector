The graph indicates data about the amount of world investments in renewable energy in period between 2006 and 2013 in developing and developed countries as far as in a whole world. Overall, the upward trends in renewable energy investment can be seen till 2011. Than it declined steadily. In order to developed and developing countries, the amount of such investments was increasing in both. As for line graph which illustrates worldwide investment in renewable energy in total world, it presents that this investment increased and reached in 2008 171 billion dollars. After a period of stagnation between 2008 and 2009 it rose dramatically and reached a peak (279 billiom dollars) in 2011. Then, it declined by 214 billion dollars in 2013. As for the data about countries, the amount of investments in renewable energy increased in both developed and developing ones with the one exception being 2009, when it declined to 100 and about 60 billion dollars in developed and developing countries respectedly. The rate of renewable energy investments was the highest in 2011 (about 170 billion dollars in developed countries and 100 in developing ones).
