Some huge companies which build cars moving their businesses in poor country help to them to build their own cars. On the other hand, it might cost a lot for their companies. They should educate workers. How to work with difficult equipment. It's expensive to build factories in this countries because of lack of resources. Moreover, citizens of these countries are less motivated to work with difficult. All in all, there are more pros than cons of this process.
