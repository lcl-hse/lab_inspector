This chart shows the proportion between boys and girls in different regions who have no opportunity to get a primary school education. 
As we can see, percent or a number of girls without bigger. In some regions these numbers have very big differences. For example, in South Asia in 2002 difference between boys and girls was 10,5 millions children. However, by 2012 the situation was changed in a goodway, number of people without education has declined seriously, both for boys and girls. 
We can see this tendency of declining in every region. Number of people without primary education has been going down during the period from 2000 to 2012. For example, in Africa it was 43,7 million children and by 2012 it is 32,7 million. The rest of the World also has this tendency.
