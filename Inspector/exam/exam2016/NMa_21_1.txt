On the gistogram below we can see the changes in school accessment in the period from 2000 to 2012 by region and gender. 
The main trend of the chart is a large degreasement of number of of children who don't have access to primary school education in South Asia from this period. (from 21,6 to 4,8 for girls, and from 11,1 to 5,1 for boys). African numbers degreased from this period too, but not so rapidly, only from 23,7 millions to 18,2 for girls and from 20 millions to 14,5 for boys).
