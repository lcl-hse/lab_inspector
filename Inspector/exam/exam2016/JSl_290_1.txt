The chart illustrates sport activities and exercise measured in time among women and men in England in 2012. Overall, the older people were, the less time they spent doing sport and exercise. The most remarkable point is that both men and women from 16 to 24 prefered to spent much more time on sport than people of other ages. Furthermore, men from 16 to 24 spent their time twice as much as women. Also, it is noticeable that there is a downward trend. The younger people were, the more time was spent on sport. Finally, it is clearly seen, that women in comparison to men spent less of their time on sport almost always. There was only one exception among people at the age of 55-64, when spent a bit more time than man. In conclusion, the time that was spent on sport and exercises decreased when people get older.
