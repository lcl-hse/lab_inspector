The graph demonstrates how much money countries spend to invest in renewable energy in the start of 21-st country. First part of graph shows amount of money invested by all countries totally. It's seen that there were periods of changes. In first one there is slight increase of investment from 100 $ bn. To 171 in the period 2006-2008. After that in 2008 was a year of stagnation, where level of investments sell a little from 171 bn to 168 $ bn. The reason of it may be in start of huge economical crysis. The next period was a time of rocket increase of investment. In 2009-2011 level of investments became in 1,5 time bigger. And in last period, from 2011 to 2013 it's seen a decrease with the same speed as a growing in previous period. From 279 $ bn to 214 $ bn. Moreover, if we watch on a second part of graph, on the boxplot we can observe that developed countries investments depend more by economic situation than developing countries. In all of shown period it is obviously seen that developed countries spend 1.5-2 times more money on researches using of renewable energy.
