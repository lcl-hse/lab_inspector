This bar chart depicts the percentage of users of three different social networks in the USA such as Facebook, Instagram and Linkedln. Facebook is very popular at usage among users of 18-29 years old and it reaches 87 percent, which is the highest in this graph. What about Instagram users of the same age, the chart is practically in the middle, which percentage is 53. Comparing the users of the age 30-49 according to these all three sites, the highest number is by Facebook, while Instagram and Linkedln are used only by 25 to 31 percent of adults. By 50-64 years old users mostly used Facebook, which amount reaches 63 percent, while instagram has the smallest amount of the users of this age. We see that people among 65 and more years are not active users of these three networks, but still percentage on Facebook, which reaches even 56 percent, is higher than on Instagram and Linkedln.
