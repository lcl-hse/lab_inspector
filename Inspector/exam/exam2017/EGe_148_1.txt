The bar chart illustrates the percentage of adults in the USA who use different social networks according to their age. 
To start with, it is clear from the graph that Facebook is more popular among young people from 18 to 29, 87% of this age group use this social network. Adults of the age from 30 to 49 and from 50 to 64 are also active users of Facebook - 73% and 63% respectively. And people of 65 years and more are not in such a favour of Facebook, only 56% of them use this network. Instagram is not as popular as Facebook but it is also mostly used by the age group of 18-29 years old. And people of the age from 30 to 49, from 50 to 64, from 65 and more use this social network very rarely (25%, 11%, 6% respectively). 
On the contrary, the number of LinkedIn users prevail among adults from 30 to 64, while only 22% of people from 18 to 29 and from 65 and more use it. 
Overall, the most popular social network is Facebook which is used by more than 50% of each age group. But the main audience for it are young people from 18 to 29 years old.
