This chart illustrates development of the book market in four countries by predicting its condition in 2018 and comparing it to 2014 data. 
Overall, in all of the countries rise of e-book market can be seen while print market either remains stable asin Germany or decreases as in three other countries Some other tendencies can be pointed out. Despite growth of e-book market, in Germany, and China printed books dominate book market both in 2014 data and in 2018 predictions. In Germany print market posseses 6 billion US and in China it posseses 4,8 billion US dollars as for 2014, while e-book market posseses only 1 billion US dollars and 0,5 billion US dollars in China. Forecast regarding both countries in 2018 show no dramatic increase of e-book market, while print market remains stable or falls slightly for 0,6 billion US dollars in China. 
In USA and UK e-book market is predicted to be dominating the book market in 2018.
