The given graph illustrates the information about using of Facebook and reasons for using it during two years from 2012 to 2013.

However, in 2013 we can notice the lowest trend of using Facebook. In March in 2012 the number of people was 55 millions of users, whereas in March in 2013 this number was 90 millions of users.
