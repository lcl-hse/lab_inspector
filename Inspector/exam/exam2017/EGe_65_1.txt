The bar chart illustrates the information about the use of Instagram, Facebook and Linkedln by americans of four age categories. 
The most striking feature of the graph is approximately 90 per cent of users of Facebook are people of 18-29 age. It is noticable that the popularity of this social network is outstanding comparing to other two and it declines to 56 per cent looking at groups of older generations. Only 6 per cent of adults in 65+ category rarely use Instagram while the number of the youngest users is as twice as big as the number of 30-49 years old people. Talking of Linkedln the difference is not that dramatic. The percentage of users fluctuates from 22 to 31 per cent, while the fourth and the first visit this site with equal frequency. 
Overall, Facebook is the most popular social network amoung people of different ages. Instagram, on the contrary, is mostly used by the group under 30 which doubles the number of visitors in the next category, while Linkedln does not have the rate of visits more than 31 per cent.
