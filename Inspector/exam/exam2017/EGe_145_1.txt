The bar chart represents the percentage of adult people in USA using such popular social networks nowadays as Facebook, Instagram and LinkedIn.
As it seen from the bar chart, all four groups of adults mostly prefer to interact with people via Facebook. Moreover, almost 90% of people between 18 and 29 use Facebook, which is the biggest amount of people involved among all of three networks. 
It is shown that only 6% of the oldest group of adults use Instagram, which is the lowest figure in the whole chart. In addition, Instagram is less popular than LinkedIn amoung people aged from 30 to 64. 
The bar chart allows us to undersdtand that social networks that encompass something more than only photos and videos, which are Facebook and LinkedIn, are more popular among people older than 30 years old. Only 22% of the youngest group use LinkedIn to communicate. Overall, Facebook can be called as the most popular social network that is used by the majority of people.
