Overall, the unemployment situation changed in various ways all over the world. But the remarkable feature of the chart is no change in worldwide unemployment rates during two periods. In North Africa there was also no change in percentage of unemployment rates. Middle East did a great job in 2015 by decreasing the rates by 1,4%. However, in Europe the rates increased but not to the critical point only by 0,4%. Moreover, Latin America was able to Implement economical tools to decrease the unemployment rates by 1%. South Asia supported the trend set by North Africa and keep its unemployment rates on the same level of 3,9%. 
To sum up, in two regions and in worldwide unemployment rates there were no changes. Middle East and Latin America decreased the rates while Europe could not do it.
