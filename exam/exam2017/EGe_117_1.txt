The bar chart illustrates the percentage of online grown ups in the United States of America by age groups who use various types of social networks. 
It can be clearly seen from the graph that young people from 18 to 29 years old use networks more often than people of other age groups. The highest percentage of use of facebook is in group of youngsters (18-29), 87% of whom prefer this site to communicate. Instagram is as well used mostly by this group of people, 53% of them are in favour of it. However, people from this age group do not use Linked In that much. It is used only by 22% of them, which is equal to the percentage of use of social networks by people older than 65 years old, who use all three types of these sites less than other groups (56% for Facebook and only 6% for Instagram). 
People by the age of 30-49 are in the second place in using social networks. 73% of them prefer facebook, 25% use Instagram and 31% goes to Linked In. In addition, Linked In is mostly used by people from this age group (31 per cent), followed by people at the age from 50 to 64 with 30% respectively. 
Overall, it is evident from the bar chart that Facebook is the most used social network as many people from different age groups use it in their everyday life.
