The graph presents an information about the usage of different social networks by the adult population of the USA. 
From the graph, we can see that Facebook and Instagram, are most popular among younger people, while LinkedIn is most popular among more mature population. Moreover, the popularity of Facebook and Instagram decreases as the age of the group increases. LinkedIn graph does not show this trend. 
Also, Facebook can be named the most popular social network in each group. 
87% of the youngest age group use Face book, 53% and 22% use Instagram and LinkedIn respectively. 
The percentage of users of Facebook falls by approximately 10% as the age of a group rises. The Instagram graph demonstrates a more abrupt fall in popularity as the age of a group increases, which can be observed in the following fact: among people of 18 - 29 years old 53% use Instagram, while among people of 30 - 49 years old 25% use the social network. As was already noticed, the changes on LinkedIn graph are more moderate. The percentage of users deviates little from the mean.
