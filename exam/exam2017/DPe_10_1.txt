The follow bar chart represents comparison of unemployment rate in some certain regions in 2014 and 2015. 
The vertical axis depicts unemployment rate and horizontal axis reflects parts of the world. 
As can be seen from the graph the lowest unemployment rate was in South Asia. It was remaining 3,9% from 2014 to 2015. The highest rate was in North Africa. It was on level of 12,5% both years. Worldwide rate stayed unchangable also. It was about 5,9% that is on two points higher from a lowest rate and practically twicely lower than highest rate of unemployment. In other regions unemployment rate fluctuated. The most considerable fluctuation was in Middle East, where rate dropped from 11 to 9,6 percent. Rate in Latin America decreased less. From 6,8% in 2014 to 5,8% in 2015. Contrary, in Europe unemployment rate in 2015 became higher. Europe is the only region on the graph that had such a situation. 
Overall unemployment rate all other the world exept one region had tendency to decrease or be at steady state.
