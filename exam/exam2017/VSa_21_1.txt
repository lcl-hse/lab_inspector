The pay charts consist information about transportation of goods by rail and by road in Eastern Europe in 2008. 
Overall, the percantage of transporting goods differ for rail and roads. 
Firstly, by rail are transportes Metals most of all, while by road are transported Food products. Almost equal number of Manufactured goods is transported by both rail and road - 25% and 28%. Similar situation is for chemicals - 11% and 16% transported by rail and road respectivly. As for Machinery, it's transported by rail six times more than by road. Non-metallic minerals transported more often by road. 
To conclude, in 2008 in Europe by rail were transported most often metals, manufactured goods, while by road were transported food products and also manufactured goods.
