These diagrams provide information about ages of population of Yemen and Italy in 2000 and suggestions about 2050. We can see, that number of old people will increase both in Italy and Yemen, but there will be small increase in Yemen, about 2 percent, while in Italy it will grow from 24,1 percent to 42,3 percent. The part of young people will change differently. It will rise from 46,3% to 57,3% in Yemen, but in Italy it will fall on one third, from 61,6% to 46,2%. Another situation with the part of children. There are projections, that number of children in Yemen will decrease significantly, from 50,1% in 2000 to 37% in 2050. In the same time, there will be a small decrease in number of children in Italy from two twentieth to one tenth of all population between 2000 and 2050. So, there are differences between changes of population in Yemen and in Italy.
