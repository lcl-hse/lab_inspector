In contrast, the proportion of people aged 65 and over in Japan had been diclining between 1940 and 1960, and remained unchanged during next 25 years, but then started to rise and was at about 27% in 2040, which was the highest percentage among the other countries. 
Overall, Sweden, USA and Japan demonstraded an upward trend in the percentage of people aged 65 and over: on Sweden and USA It increased steadily, in Japan it had been declining and then increased dramatically.
