Following graphs represent the data about the populations' ages of both Yemen and Italy on the period of time since 2000 and up to 2050. 
Overally, there are 4 pie charts which are divided into 3 sections. Each of them merely describes are out of three groups with different age. It is considered that in Yemen the percentage of 0-14 years population will decrease by 37%, while 15-59 years group will be described with increased 57,3% and the amount of 60+ aged people will increase in 2,1%. Reverse trend can be observed in Italy. Progressing with the time, the multitude of teenagers and adults will narrows as well as the number of 60+ aged people, although, the percentage of children will decrease. 
So this is the main features of considering pie charts.
