The first chart compares the number of unique visitors of Facebook in March 2012 and March 2013, while the second illustrates the reasons why men and women use it. Overall, it is clear from the firth cart that total amount of users (both desktop and mobile) increased from around 205 millions to 220 millions. But that happened only due to significant growth in mobile user, while the amount of desktop monthly visitors slightly fell over a year. The second chart shows that more than a half of women consider sharing media content to be the main reason of using Facebook. At the same time around 40 percent of both men and women name receiving updates among other key reasons. To conclude, the trend of moving from desktop to mobile is evident from the chart. In addition to it, women are more engaged in the way they use Facebook while men are more likely to name different reasons for visiting it that are not reflected in the data.
