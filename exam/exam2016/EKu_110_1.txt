The given two charts below show changes in the use of Facebook from 2012 to 2013 and the main reasons for using it among men and women. As we can see, in the March of 2012 and in the March of 2013 the proportion of people using Facebook on the desktops are on the same level (near 140 millions of users). But in the same time the percentage of people using Facebook on the mobile phones is increased rapidly and in the beginning of the year of 2013 it reaches more than 95 millions of users. Most of women like to surf the Facebook for sharing photos and videos with others (more than a half of total number) and there is a not so high percentage for men -- 39%. It is interesting that both men and women are receivivg updates on Facebook at the same proportion -- 39 %. But also women's one of the main reason to use this social network is to see funny posts and, what is unpredictable, men do it less. And if we are talking about learning about how to help others, it is a reason for surfing the Facebook for a gourth of men. For women this percentage reaches 35 %.
