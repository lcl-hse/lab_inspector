As for Rest of World, as can be seen, number of girls without access to primary school dropped and achieved a level with only 7.6 million which is less than amount of boys with 7.7 millions. To sum up, the leader with the smallest amount of children without access to primary school in 2002 was Rest of World. However, in 2012 the new leader appeared and it was South Asia.
