The given chart illustrates how many children did not have access to primary education in different regions and paying attention on gender. It can be seen in the chart that in Africa both in 2000 and 2012 girls didn't have education in most of case. In addition, the number of children who did not go to school reduced from approximately 20 million in 2000 in every gender to 18,2 million girls and 14,5 million boys. This trend can be found in South Asia. Moreover, in South Asia in 2012 more boys than girls was without access to primary education. On the rest of world less children did not have education than in Africa both in 2000 and 2012 and South Asia in 2000. Nevertheless, in 2012 in rest of world there are more non-education children than in South Asia. What is the interesting is the fact than in 2012 in South Asia and rest of world there are more million boys without education. Overall, the number of children which did not have primary education were reduced from 2000 to 2012.
