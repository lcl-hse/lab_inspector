The following data demonstrates statistics about using one of the most famous networks, Facebook. The first chart concentrates on the amount of users that changed over a year, while the second illustrates the purposes for being online. According to the presented information, from 2012 to 2013 the amount of people who used Facebook through their desktops declined by 20 millions. Controversely, the usage of Facebook through mobile devices increased significantly from aproximately 55 millions to almost 100 millions of people. The statistics also indicates that more than a half of women uses Facebook for sharing photos and videos, and more than a third reads funny posts, receves updates and learns about ways to help others through this social net. Men show slightly less enthusiasm about the purposes concidered to be "main reasons". For instance, only 39% of them propound sharing photos and receiving updates to be the main reason for using Facebook, and only a quater keeps in mind the ways to help others while using Facebook. Overall, according to the presented data, over the following period people prefered using mobile devices to pass their time in Facebook rather then desktops and, concerning percentage, women agree with the following reasons for using Facebook more often than men.
